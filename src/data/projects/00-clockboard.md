ClockBoard is a canvas-rendered React component that drives a 20x8 wall of analog clocks. It drifts, spins, and shows the time on its own — every viewing is different, almost as if it's alive.

It may look familiar to frequent flyers from the Bay Area...

Turn a TV or monitor into a decorative art piece for free. Buying a physical version of this could cost you $10k+. You can also use it as a clock or graphic element for your React projects.

ClockBoard is fully [open source](https://github.com/jeffreyjy/clockboard). You can also view it on [npm](https://www.npmjs.com/package/clockboard). To add it to your project:
```bash
pnpm add clockboard
```
