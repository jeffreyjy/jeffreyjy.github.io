ClockBoard is a React component that renders a 20x8 wall of analog clocks and synchronizes their hands to create animated visual patterns over time. The grid can display ambient motion, stylized digit layouts, and time-based compositions while preserving smooth animation performance in the browser.

It may look familiar to frequent flyers from the Bay Area...

ClockBoard can be used to turn a TV or monitor into a decorative art piece for free. Buying a physical version of this could cost you $10k+. You can also use it as a neat clock or graphic for your react websites and projects. 

ClockBoard is fully [open source](https://github.com/jeffreyjy/clockboard). You can also view it on [npm](https://www.npmjs.com/package/clockboard). To add it to your project, simply run:
```bash
pnpm add clockboard
```
